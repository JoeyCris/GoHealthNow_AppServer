Thank you very much for trying and using GoHealthNow. We want to share with you a few new and exciting developments in recent months.

First of all, new versions (both Android and iOS) of GoHealthNow have been released in early September. Please update it soon.

In the new version, texts are larger with high contrast, so they are easy for you to see.

Also food recognition from photos works in Auto-Estimate of the Diet input. Though it can only recognize 40 or so types of popular North American dishes, we are constantly improving and expanding. It provides a fun and easy way to log your meal and snacks, with an estimated nutritional values, which you can always manually adjust. If we cannot recognize your food, please use Search with keywords to get accurate nutrition information.

Another great feature of GoHealthNow is its Online Logbook. Go to https://myaccount.glucoguide.com

from a computer or tablet for a complete overview of your data, charts and trends. You can see a Demo Logbook there! Do not use your smartphones as the screens are too small. You can print your logbook to bring to your healthcare providers!
